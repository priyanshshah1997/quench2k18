<ol type="disc" align="left">

4. If a team is found cheating, they will be immediately eliminated<br>

<h4>Rounds</h4>
<ol>
<h4>Rounds:</h4>
<br><h4>Round 1:  Weeding Out</h4>
1. Elimination round based on student’s capability to prove the given Electronics
laws using proteus Software.<br>
2. The circuit simplicity and efficient output in less time will be the judging
criteria.<br>
<h4>Round 2: Best Out of Waste<h4>
1. Qualifying teams have to make a moving model from the junk provided.<br>
2. In this round the teams will compete for the race among the models they have
made.<br>
3. The first reaching the finish line will qualify for the next round.<br>
<h4>Round 3: Crowning the legends</h4>
1. Teams reaching the final round will have to make an electronic circuit from the
electronic components provided.<br>
2. The circuit simplicity, innovative ideas, effective outputs in given time will lead to the
winning position.<br>
<h4>Round 4: Implement it</h4>
1. Teams reaching the final round will have to implement the circuit made in round 3 on the moving model made in round 2.
<br>

</ol>
