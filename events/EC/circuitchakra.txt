<h4>General Rules</h4><br>
<ol type="disc" align="left">
1. A team of 3 members can participate (all from same college).<br>
2. Use of electronic gadgets is prohibited.<br>
3. All rounds are time bound. Earlier you finish properly, more points
you earn.<br>

</ol>
<h4>Rounds</h4>
<ol>
<h4>Rounds:</h4>
<br><h4>Round 1:  What's in your Wallet.</h4>
Duration:  Maximum 3 hours.<br>
1. On the basis of clues, teams will hunt and earn points.<br>
2. Earlier you hunt, more points you earn.<br>
3. Tougher the clue, more you earn.<br>
4. Points earned will be useful to purchase components in next level.<br>
5. Top 15 teams would qualify for next round.<br>

<h4>Round 2: Get you Own Box.<h4>
Duration:  Maximum 3 hours.<br>
1. On the basis of points earned in round 1, teams can purchase components.<br>
2. Design a working circuit from the components.<br>
3. Best 8 innovative designs go to the next round.<br>
<h4>Round 2: Jo Bikega, Wo Chalega<h4>
Duration:  Maximum 3 hours.<br>
1. Qualifying teams will now sell their circuit within college using them marketing skills.<br>
2. Usefulness of the circuit, innovation, marketing skills and team work will be
the judging criteria.<br>
3. Best two teams will be chosen.<br>

</ol>
