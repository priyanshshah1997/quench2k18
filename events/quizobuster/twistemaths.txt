<h4>General Rules</h4><br>
<ol type="disc" align="left">
1. If any of the board is being damaged by participant intentionally than he/she will
be disqualified.<br>
2. No external help will be permitted.<br>
3. Use of pen and paper is not permitted to give the answer.<br>
4. Rule of DMAS ( Division, Multiplication, Addition, and Subtraction ) is not
applicable to calculate the answer in the first round.<br>
5. If the participant is found damaging the board or any of the blocks then he/she
will be disqualified.<br>
6. For healthy and successful completion of an event, coordinators have right to
change the rules and regulations on the spot. Any argument against it won't be
entertained.<br>
</ol>
<br>
<h4>Rounds</h4>
<ol align="left">
It will consist of three rounds.<br>
1. In this round a question will be given to participants and participant has to get the correct
answer by placing the correct math operator.<br>
2. It’s all about logic here and consists of two games.<br>
a. Unblock Me: In this round each participant has to unblock the given block by moving
the block horizontally or vertically.<br>
b. Sokoban: In this participant has to move the given block in its mark position on the
given board.<br>
3. Sudoku: In this round the participant has to solve the given Sudoku puzzle in given amount of
time.<br>
</ol>
